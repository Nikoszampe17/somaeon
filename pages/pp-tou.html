This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you visit our website or contact us to book a wellness or body treatment session. We use your personal data exclusively to respond to your inquiries, communicate with you regarding wellness or body treatment sessions, and organize and provide the requested service. Your data is not used for marketing purposes, newsletters, or automated decision-making.

The legal basis for processing your data under GDPR is your consent, given when submitting the contact form, and pre-contractual necessity, as the data is required to arrange the requested service. You may withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us.

We do not collect or store payment card details on our website. Stripe acts as an independent data controller for payment processing and complies with industry security standards and data protection laws. For more information, please refer to Stripe’s Privacy Policy.
