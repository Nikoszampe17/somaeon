Somaeon is a unique and original name derived from two core ideas:

  • "Soma" (σῶμα) — an ancient greek word used in Greek tradition, meaning “body” but also referring to a sacred elixir or essence of well-being.
  • "Aeon" (αἰών) — a word meaning timelessness, eternal life, or a long span of time.

Together, Somaeon represents: “A timeless journey through the essence of the body and soul.”

It captures the harmony between tradition and modernity, grounding the brand in ancient wisdom while pointing to a serene, holistic future.
