Become a Somaeon Host

Offer your guests something they will truly remember — curated in-home wellness rituals, delivered directly to their stay.

Why partner with Somaeon

Premium guest experience

Transform your stay into more than a place to sleep. Provide your guests with spa-level rituals in the comfort of their temporary home.

Zero operational weight

No equipment to store, no planning, no logistics. We coordinate everything directly with your guests, from booking to arrival.

Better reviews & repeat stays

Wellness experiences consistently translate into higher ratings, stronger word-of-mouth and a stay that feels distinctly memorable.

How it works

01

Guests discover the service

You include Somaeon in your listing, digital guidebook or welcome materials as an optional in-home wellness experience.

02

They request a session

Guests reach out using your dedicated contact line or our booking page, mentioning your property as their host.

03

We handle the rest

A licensed therapist arrives fully equipped, and your guests enjoy a curated Somaeon ritual — with no extra work on your end.

Host benefits

  • Exclusive host pricing and access to special offers for your guests.
  • Optional revenue share structure for selected partners.
  • Priority calendar access during high season and peak dates.
  • Guest-ready digital materials (QR codes, info cards, copy for listings).
  • Suggestions tailored to long-stay or corporate guests.
  • Direct line with Somaeon for smooth coordination.

What we look for in a host

  • A clean, quiet room with enough space for a massage table.
  • Basic comfort: temperature control and the ability to dim or adjust lighting.
  • Clear communication and responsiveness regarding guests’ wellness requests.
  • The ability to transparently present Somaeon as an optional in-home wellness partner in your listing or welcome guide.
